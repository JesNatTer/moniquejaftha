About me

My name is Monique Jaftha, a 25 year old certified Freelance Journalist and co-author of several poetry anthologies. In 2021, my article on best-selling author Nicholas Sparks was published on The Writer's College Times. I started writing at the age of 14 and have been chronicling my journey through life, ever since. I had the opportunity to learn from young climate change and children's rights activists during the ERth Summit in October. I enjoy learning new things or finding a new perspective to look at things. I am able to multitask and prioritise things accordingly.
